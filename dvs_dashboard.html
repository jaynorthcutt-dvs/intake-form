Your enrollment link

          
Share this personal link (or QR code) with clients. Anyone who enrolls through it is routed to you as their agent of record.

Your referral link

          
Share this link (or QR code) with your own referral partners. When they refer a client through it, the client is routed to you as the agent of record and the partner is recorded as the referral source.

          

            
            
          

          

            
            
Your referral partners can scan this to open a referral form tied to you. Right-click the code to save it for sharing.
